No Party for Cao Dong  (草东没有派对)

No Party For Cao Dong, formerly known as Cao Dong street Party, is a Chinese Taiwan rock and roll band composed of lead singer and guitarist wu du, guitarist zhu zhu, bassist shi xuan and drummer fan fan.

In 2012, the grass east street party was officially established. In 2014, caodong street party was renamed caodong no party, and successively published Lao zhang, Chou and other works on the Internet. In 2015, the band shot its first music video, mountain sea. In 2016, the band released their debut music album ugly nuer. In the same year, the "dong shui bing shen year winter" tour was held. On June 24, 2017, "the wind blows" won the song of the year at the 28th Taiwan golden melody awards for best new artist and best orchestra.
